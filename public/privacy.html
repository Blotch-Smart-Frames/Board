Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2025

Board by Blotch ("we", "our", or "the app") is a task management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store the following information from your Google account:

  • Name — your display name, used to identify you within shared boards.
  • Email address — used for account identification and board collaboration invitations.
  • Profile photo URL — displayed as your avatar within the app.

We also store data you create within the app, including boards, lists, and cards.

If you enable Google Calendar sync, we access your Google Calendar data using the calendar and calendar.events OAuth scopes. This allows us to read, create, update, and delete calendar events linked to your tasks. We only access calendar events that are created or managed by Board by Blotch.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the Board by Blotch service:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session.
  • Display your profile to collaborators on shared boards.
  • Enable collaboration features such as board sharing and assignee selection.
  • Store and retrieve your boards, lists, and cards.
  • Sync tasks with your Google Calendar when you enable calendar sync — creating, updating, and removing calendar events to reflect your task due dates.
  • Read changes from your Google Calendar to keep linked tasks up to date (e.g. if you reschedule an event in Google Calendar, the task's due date is updated accordingly).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), which provides encryption at rest and in transit. We do not store your Google password. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's OAuth 2.0 service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be visible to:

  • Other users you explicitly invite to collaborate on a board.
  • Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure as our hosting and database provider.

5. Third-Party Services

Board by Blotch uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and hosting.
  • Google OAuth 2.0 — sign-in authentication.
  • Google Calendar API — two-way sync between tasks and calendar events.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Google API Services Usage Disclosure

Board by Blotch's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request access to the Google Calendar scopes that are necessary to provide calendar sync functionality.
  • We do not use Google Calendar data for advertising, market research, or any purpose unrelated to providing the app's task-to-calendar sync feature.
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google Calendar data unless it is necessary to provide support you have requested, we have your explicit consent, or we are required to do so by law.
  • We do not transfer Google Calendar data to third parties except as necessary to provide the service (via Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure) or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app.
  • Delete your boards, lists, and cards at any time.
  • Revoke access by removing Board by Blotch from your Google account's connected apps at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Request full account deletion by contacting us at the email below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Board by Blotch does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Firebase Authentication may use local storage or session cookies solely for maintaining your authenticated session.

9. Children's Privacy

Board by Blotch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
